    Saffron is harvested from the stigmas of the

    one of the most expensive spices in the world.

    Harvesting Process


    Saffron is hand-harvested from the crocus sativus flower.
    Each flower products only three red stigmas, which are
    carefully picked by hand to ensure they remain .
    It takes 15,000 flowers to products just one kilogram of saffron in the world.
